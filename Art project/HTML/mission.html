Parent Advisory Committee ― BCMP's Parent Advisory Committee (PAC) is comprised of parents who facilitate fundraising efforts for the school. All parents of BCMP students are eligible for one of the five elected representative positions (chairperson, vice-chairperson, treasurer, secretary, and fundraiser coordinator), and all parents are encouraged to attend monthly PAC meetings. If interested in becoming involved with the PAC at your child's school, please contact the school directly and ask for more information.
